Create an image in gimp with 7 "layers"
----------------------------------------
original image: jms-icon.ico
- open gimp
- open denis_jms_icon.xcf
- 1 layer per image required


export as ".ico"
Check:
- 3 images in 8 bits
- 4 in 32bits
! Must all be uncompressed

From scratch:
- open gimp
- open jms-icon.ico
- duplicate layer 6 times
- rename each layer
- on each layer, right click and rescale
